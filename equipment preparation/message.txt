Recommended Camera Roles
Camera	Role
DJI Pocket 4 #1	Main speaker tracking and medium/close-up shots
DJI Pocket 4 #2	Second speaker, side angle, presentation, or reaction shots
GoPro MAX2	Full 360 safety recording and AI-generated virtual camera angles
Recommended Workflow
Stage	Workflow
Live production	Pocket 4 cameras → USB-C UVC → Computer → AI Director → OBS
Audio processing	DJI Mic → Computer → Active-speaker detection and transcription
360 recording	GoPro MAX2 → Local 8K recording on microSD
Post-production	MAX2 footage → AI tracking and reframing → Automatic highlights and final edit
Final output	OBS recording + Pocket 4 files + MAX2 virtual shots + subtitles and social clips

Important: The Pocket 4 cameras should be used for real-time AI input. The GoPro MAX2 should mainly record the full scene for post-production because its 360 footage is ideal for creating virtual camera angles after the event.
